About Us

Agape Academy International serves students aged 3 to 18, encompassing pre-school to senior high school levels. The academic year is divided into four terms, running from August to June. Beyond academics, the school offers a vibrant extracurricular program featuring activities such as choirs, modern dance, ballet, piano lessons, sports, creativity, drama, and chess.
